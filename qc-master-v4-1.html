📐 4-Point Fabric Inspection Guide (sirf reference ke liye — print mein nahi aayega)

Minor, chhoti kharabi
🟠

3 Points

4 Points
9+ inch defect

✅ PASS Rule
100 meter fabric mein
40 points se kam = PASS ✅
Formula: Total Points ÷ (Length × Width/36) × 100
❌ REJECT Rule
100 meter fabric mein
40 points ya zyada = REJECT ❌
Supplier ko wapas karein ya discount negotiate karein
📌 Yaad rakhein: Ek full-width defect (selvage to selvage) = 4 Points automatically, chahe size kitna bhi chhota ho. Har meter mein sirf max 4 points diye ja sakte hain.

🧮 Quick AQL Calculator
📋 AQL 1.5
High-end / premium products ke liye. Bahut strict standard. Minor defect bhi acceptable nahi.
📋 AQL 2.5
Industry standard. Garments aur home textiles ke liye sabse zyada use hota hai.
📋 AQL 4.0
Basic / economy products. Thoda zyada defect acceptable. Inner wear, basic items.
📌 Yaad rakhein: AQL ka matlab hai ki agar aap 100 shipments check karein to utnay % shipments mein defect allowed hai. AQL 2.5 = 2.5% se kam defect rate acceptable. Sample mein "Accept" number se zyada defective milein to poori lot reject karein ya 100% inspection karein.
